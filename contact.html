Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does a typical AI project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. Simple analytics projects may take 4-8 weeks, while comprehensive AI implementations can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have deep expertise in agriculture, fintech, healthcare, retail, and logistics. However, our team has successfully delivered projects across many other industries. We adapt our solutions to meet specific industry requirements.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support packages including system monitoring, updates, training, and optimization. Our support plans are tailored to your specific needs and can include 24/7 monitoring for critical systems.

Can you work with our existing technology stack?

Absolutely. We're experienced in integrating with various technology stacks and platforms. Our team will assess your current infrastructure and design solutions that work seamlessly with your existing systems.

What makes Axile different from other AI consulting firms?

Our combination of deep technical expertise, industry knowledge, and proven track record with organizations like CSIRO and ACIAR sets us apart. We focus on delivering practical, scalable solutions that drive real business value.
